Popular Book Genres

Mystery

A genre of fiction that usually involves a mysterious death or a crime to be solved. Mystery novels are often called 'whodunnits' because they turn the reader into a detective trying to figure out the who, when, and how of a particular crime.

Science Fiction

A genre of speculative fiction that typically deals with imaginative and futuristic concepts such as advanced science and technology, space exploration, time travel, and extraterrestrial life.

Dystopian

Dystopian literature is a form of hypothetical fiction that began as a response to utopian literature. A dystopia is an imagined community or society that is dehumanizing and frightening.
